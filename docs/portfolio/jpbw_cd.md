# JPBW Corporate Design <!-- {docsify-ignore-all} -->

The initial challenge was to design a logo for the Jugendpresse BW [Jugendpresse BW](https://jpbw.de/ ':target=_blank') (JPBW, a student press association), an event they manage called Jugendmedientage, and their 30th anniversary. To that end, I used colorful and versatile facets that visualize the creativity and diversity of the JPBW's work. I compiled the main design aspects of the logo(s) into a corporate design manual, which now serves as the basis for all publications.

Freelance work, 2013.
