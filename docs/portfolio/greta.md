# Greta Thunberg's Transatlantic Voyage Social Media Campaign <!-- {docsify-ignore-all} -->

In August 2019, Greta Thunberg sailed across the Atlantic Ocean from Plymouth, England, to New York City. There, she attended the UN Climate Action Summit. For a social media campaign in support of her journey, I created shareable images that raise awareness about the severity of the climate crisis by presenting scientific facts.

Freelance work for [part](https://part.berlin/ ':target=_blank'), 2019.
