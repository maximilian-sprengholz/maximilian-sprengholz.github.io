# vvolt Magazine <!-- {docsify-ignore-all} -->

In 2015, [SchildDirect](https://www.schilddirect.de/ ':target=_blank') and I explored the possibility of creating a magazine for electricians. We published and distributed 20,000 copies of the first issue in January 2016. However, we could not secure enough subscriptions and advertisements to keep the magazine running.

Freelance work, design made with [Timm Hartmann](https://www.ynm.studio/de/ ':target=_blank'), 2015-2016.
