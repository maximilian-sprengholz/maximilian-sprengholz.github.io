# SPD.de Relaunch <!-- {docsify-ignore-all} -->

Tough Challenge: We transformed the former colossus into a colorful, lightweight, easy-to-use website — the most modern German party homepage. It combines clear political positions with interactive elements, such as support forms and social media sharing buttons, to generate buzz. Shortlisted for the [Deutscher Preis für Onlinekommunikation](https://www.dpok.de/rueckblick/best-of-2016/#22-website ':target=_blank').

Concept and design made at [BUTTERBERLIN](http://www.butterberlin.com ':target=_blank'), implementation by Pixelpark, 2016.
