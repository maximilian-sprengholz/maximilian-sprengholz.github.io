# SWR1 CLP Campaign <!-- {docsify-ignore-all} -->

This is a city light poster design for the German radio station SWR1. Real records were laser-etched according to vector data and photographed. The slogan translates to "Our world is a disc." According to SWR1, they have never received better feedback from their listeners regarding a campaign.

Design made at [fischerAppelt Stuttgart](http://www.fischerappelt.de ':target=_blank') with [Davide Di Donna](http://be.net/DavideDiDonna ':target=_blank') and [Jedrzej Golecki](https://www.behance.net/jtgolecki9ae3 ':target=_blank'), 2013.
